[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the heat when you look my way
A quiet burn at the end of the day
Your shadow dances on the wooden floor
Leaving me hungry and begging for more

[Verse 1]
The moonlight spills across your skin
I am ready for the night to begin
Just the rhythm of our breathing here
Pulling you close, drawing you near

[Pre-Chorus]
A lingering glance, a dangerous smile
Let's lock the door and get lost for a while
The midnight air is holding its breath
Waiting for the sweetness of a soft caress

[Chorus]
Oh, your touch is a slow and beautiful flame
Whispering secrets that I cannot name
We are tangled up in the evening glow
Take my hand and never let it go

[Post-Chorus]
Just you and me, letting the hours slip
With the taste of forever right on my lip

[Verse 2]
The candlelight flickers on your collarbone
I just want to get you completely alone
I trace the lines along your open palm
Finding my storm inside your quiet calm

[Pre-Chorus 2]

[Chorus]

[Post-Chorus 2]

With the taste of forever right on my lip

[Bridge]
The shadows fall and the world fades away
We leave behind the rush of the day
You pull me close with that look in your eye
I am completely yours under the sky
Yeah, it's Saeed, and I'm caught in your flame
I will keep holding you, never the same

[Spoken]
(You know the way you look at me right now)
(It makes the whole world stop turning)

[Final Chorus]
Oh, your touch is a slow and beautiful flame
Whispering secrets that I cannot name
